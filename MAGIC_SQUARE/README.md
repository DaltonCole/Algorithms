# Magic Square

This program creates a magic square of n size where n != 2

## Compile

g++ -std=c+=11 *.cpp

## Input

The desired size of the square is read in.

### Example

```
6
```
## Output

The magic constant (the sum of each row, column, and diagnal) is outputed followed by the magic square.

### Example

```
Magic Number: 111
35 	1 	6 	26 	19 	24 	
3 	32 	7 	21 	23 	25 	
31 	9 	2 	22 	27 	20 	
8 	28 	33 	17 	10 	15 	
30 	5 	34 	12 	14 	16 	
4 	36 	29 	13 	18 	11 
```
